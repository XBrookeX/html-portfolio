The Best Movies According to Brooke

My Top 3 Movies/Shows of All-Time.

How To Train Your Dragon 2

Without a doubt, the best animated movie of all time. The story and animation is top tier.

Avatar

The best movie of all time. The visual effects and acting skills can't be beaten.

Naruto

Arguably, the best OG anime of all time. Not too long like one piece but more original than Dragon Ball. Truly the best of the Big 3.
